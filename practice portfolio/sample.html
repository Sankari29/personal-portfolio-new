Project 1

Cervical Cancer Multilevel Classification By using CNN Techniques

The Cervical Cancer Multilevel Classification project utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to accurately classify cervical cancer stages based on medical imaging data. The model is trained to recognize patterns and abnormalities in cervical images, enabling it to distinguish between different stages of cancer. Using multilevel classification, the system categorizes images into multiple stages, providing a deeper insight into the severity of the condition. The project demonstrates my ability to apply deep learning techniques in medical diagnostics, specifically for image classification, and highlights the use of CNNs for precise, automated medical analysis.
